Profile

Anoop is currently a Security Consultant in EY's Managed Detection Response team who loves to explore the Cyber World. He is a developer who loves playing around SIEM, UEBA, Web Apps and Linux. He graduated from Cochin University of Science and Technology, Information Technology Program with First Class and Distinction.

He is passionate about Cyber Security and holds CEH, RHCSA, NSA1, NSA2 certification. He has knowledge in multiple domains Cloud Computing, Vulnerability Assessment, Incident Response, Threat Intelligence and Threat Management.

He is also deeply involved in the community; He is a Tech Speaker and has taken sessions for Developers and Professionals. He expresses himself via talks and his blogs which he publishes in Medium, Hackernoon and his personal blog. He loves Opensource and mentors student developers every year to get started with opensource in nation-wide community events . Beside Information Security, he enjoys watching anime, doing outdoor activities and connecting with new people!
